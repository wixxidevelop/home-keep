Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How fast is your delivery?

Our average delivery time is 12 minutes for fuel delivery, and we guarantee delivery within 15 minutes during business hours. For other services, delivery times vary but we always aim for same-day or next-day service.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Benin City and surrounding areas within a 25km radius. This includes major residential areas, commercial districts, and industrial zones. Contact us to confirm if we serve your specific location.

How do I make a payment?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Payment is due upon delivery of service. For regular customers, we also offer monthly billing options.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

For fuel delivery, we require a minimum order of 10 litres to qualify for free delivery. For other services like laundry and gas refill, there's no minimum order requirement.

Are your services available on weekends?

Yes! We operate 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We also provide emergency services 24/7 for urgent fuel delivery needs.

How do I track my order?

Once you place an order, we'll send you live updates via WhatsApp or SMS. You'll receive notifications when your order is confirmed, dispatched, and when our delivery person is nearby.
